# 28-Day Roadmap

The plan moves from B1+/B2- reliability to comfortable B2 control. The focus gradually shifts from accuracy and correction to fluency, natural phrasing, and examiner-ready answers.

## Progression Logic

- Days 1 to 7 establish the learner's baseline and stabilize recurring errors.
- Days 8 to 14 expand controlled output into work, education, and technology topics.
- Days 15 to 21 increase complexity, nuance, and the ability to defend opinions naturally.
- Days 22 to 28 focus on examiner performance, fluency, and high-quality B2 phrasing.
