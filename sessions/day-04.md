# Day 4

## Theme

Technology in daily life.

## Grammar Focus

Comparatives and modifiers.

## Warm-Up Correction

Correct these sentences:

1. My new phone is much more easier to use than my old one.
2. This app is more efficient than the other app, but it is not the most convenient one.
3. Online meetings are becoming more and more popular in modern work.

## Reading Task

Read this short text:

> Many people now rely on digital tools for work, study, and entertainment. A good app can save time, reduce stress, and improve productivity. However, not every new device is better than the old one. In some cases, simpler tools are more reliable and easier to use. The best choice depends on the user's needs, budget, and daily habits.

Answer these questions:

1. What are three benefits of digital tools mentioned in the text?
2. Why are simpler tools sometimes a better choice?

## Vocabulary

Use these items in your answers if possible:

- efficient
- convenient
- reliable
- user-friendly
- affordable
- advanced
- practical
- productivity

## Writing Task

Write 90-110 words.

Prompt: Compare a smartphone and a laptop for daily use. Which one is more useful for you, and why?

## Speaking Task

Answer in 1-2 minutes.

Prompt: Compare two technology tools you use often.

Follow-up questions:

1. Which one saves more time?
2. Which one is more convenient?
3. Would you choose a cheaper or more advanced version?

## Listening Task

Optional: Listen for the main idea in a short technology news clip and summarize it in one sentence.

## Evaluation Notes

- Focus on natural comparative structures.
- Use modifiers carefully: much, a lot, slightly, far, more and less.
- Avoid repeated adjective patterns such as "more easier".

## Memory Update

- Add any new technology vocabulary.
- Record any new comparative or modifier mistakes.

## Wrap-Up

### Gemini Evaluation

- Vocabulary use: mostly accurate, with verb-form errors in "comes with" and "games I am playing."
- Speaking: coherent and natural overall, but with a few form errors such as "Nowadays" and "it saves much more time."
- Listening: main idea and key detail were correctly identified.
- Final verdict: B2.

### Key Corrections

- ❌ The new laptop model is come with.
	✅ The new laptop model comes with.
- ❌ Nowaday.
	✅ Nowadays.
- ❌ it save much more time.
	✅ it saves much more time.
